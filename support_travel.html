A list of never ending landmarks

RamaKrishna Beach:


Popularly known as The RK Beach,is probably the go-to destination for every Vizagitie, let it be a family outing or a birthday celebration,RK Beach spreads both of its arms wide. It is the most visited and famous spot in Vizag

Lammasingi:


Located in the Chintapalli Agency,It records the lowest temperatures in vizag,Wanna get chilled(literally),There's no place better than this!Be prepared to pack your gear as it involves a lot of ghat travel.

Borra Caves:


One of the major attractions when you are on your way to Araku,with its pre-historic carvings,its definitely a worth watch. You can always have a stop at this enticing spot on your way to Araku

Zoo-Park:


Hey Animal Lovers! Vizag never disappoints you. with a vast area of over 2000hectares,Indira Gandhi zoological park is a home for wide variety of species from all over the world

Kailasagiri:


Planning for a Picnic? We bet there's no other place better than this.with its cheerful ambience and breath-taking views,it thouroughly satisfies you! Best for a hang-out with family.



Simhachalam:


How can a tour be complete without the lord's darshan! Simhachalam located in a hill range is very sacred and people visit it from all over the country with an average visitor count of 10000 per day
